History Of the Parish

The St.Marys Orthodox Syrian Church, Vasco da Gama had a humble beginning in the year 1966 under the spiritual guidance and leadership of late Rev. Fr. Philip Pallichira . The Orthodox Syrian Christians numbering about 15 along with few members of other Christian denominations hailing from Kerala and employed in the port town of Vasco Da Gama had been assembling as a unique congregation.
The earnest desire of this congregation was to have a worship place of their own became a reality when a small chapel was constructed in December 1972 on a piece of land secured by them in Varunapuri adjacent to Naval Establishment. The Christmas Service of 1972 was conducted in the new chapel by Rev.Fr. Philp Pallichara. Rev. Fr. T.E Issac was appointed as his successor. Thereafter the Metropolitian of the Bombay Diocese posted Rev. Fr. John Jacob Puthenpurayil, a senior priest as resident vicar of the parish . Under the able leadership and guidance of Rev. Fr. John Jacob Puthenpurayil, the construction of the church building was completed successfully in January 1992. The renovated church was finally consecrated on 16/2/1992 at the hands of Late His Grace Philipose Mar Theophilose Metrolpolitan of the Bombay Diocese.
Rev.Fr. John Jacob Puthenpurayil had to retire as vicar of this parish with effect from May ,1998 due to his ill health. Rev.Fr. T.U Thomas was appointed as his successor from June 1988 to Sept 1999. Rev.Fr. Geevarghese Iype was appointed as his successor till Sept 2001. Followed by we had Rev. Fr. Abraham Mathew was appointed as the vicar till the year 2003. Then we had Rev. Fr. Laby George till the year 2005. After that we had Rev. Fr. George Vargheese till the year 2007. Rev. Fr. Joshua O.I.C was was appointed as the vicar till the year 2009. Then we had Rev Fr. K.M Oommen till the year 2015 followed by Rev. Fr. Philipose Mathew was appointed as the vicar with effect from July 2015 till 8th Novemember 2015. Now Rev.Fr. Geevarghese Varghese is appointed as the vicar in his place with effect from November 15th 2015. Thank You for visiting us online. We welcome you to visit the church and join us in worship services. Our mass is celebrated according to the ancient traditions of the Syrian Orthodox Church in Malayalam-Syriac-languages.

St. Marys Orthodox Church

The St. Marys Orthodox Church is situated in the heart of the city of Vasco, one of the Principal cities of the state of Goa. Our Church is located just a stones throw away from the Goa Airport .
