Welcome

We welcome you to the First Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church Parish in Goa. It's an amazing grace to have you visit our website. Our church belongs to The Diocese of Brahmavor of the Malankara orthodox Syrian Church.
the st. marys orthodox syrian church is situated in the heart of city of vasco, one of the principal cities of the state of Goa. Our church is located just a stones throw away from the Goa Airport.
we will be delighted to have you visit our church and join us in worship services. May the grace of God be with you and to your family during your visit here.

History Of the Parish

The St.Marys Orthodox Syrian Church, Vasco da Gama had a humble beginning in the year 1966 under the spiritual guidance and leadership of late Rev. Fr. Philip Pallichira . The Orthodox Syrian Christians numbering about 15 along with few members of other Christian denominations hailing from Kerala and employed in the port town of Vasco Da Gama had been assembling as a unique congregation.

Brahmavar Diocese

Brahmavar (Goan) Orthodox Church is a uniate faction from the Catholic Church formed under the leadership of Bishop Antonio Francisco Xavier Alvares in 1889.Antonio Francisco Xavier Alvares was consecrated as the first Orthodox Metropolitan of Goa-Ceylon in 1889 AD by Paulose Mar Athanasious and Gheevarghese Mar Gregorios of Parumala at the Orthodox Pazhaya Seminary.
